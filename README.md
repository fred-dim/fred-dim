### About Me
As a trained finance and ESG enthusiast, I have recently sharpened my data science skills in a 9-week full-time coding bootcamp. Within the scope of our final project, we developed a cloud-hosted application in a team of four. Fetching up-to-date Sentinel satellite imagery, the app tracks deforestation in the Amazon region by means of state-of-the-art image segmentation (model of choice: U-Net with integrated attention mechanism). 

Now, I strive to combine my domain knowledge with my data proficiency. Thereby, I am particularly interested in learning more about Advanced SQL, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.
